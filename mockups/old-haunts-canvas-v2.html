Your two anchors. Flow 1 = map → dynamic tour with a ghost guide. Flow 2 = camera → photo → rewind → must talk. The other four remix these.
Memories = the shareable asset. Every conversation + rewound photo is auto-saved as a replayable card. This is what people post after — the app’s viral loop.
Steal from the field (Talking Statues, Streetmuseum, Timelooper):
① It calls YOU. The statue phoning you beats hunting for content — surprise > search. Add “an old haunt is calling…” when you’re near.
② Spatial lock = motivation — unlock only on-site (QR fallback for bad GPS).
③ Tease the reveal — it wakes, clears its throat, then speaks. The transition sells the magic.
④ Replay = the social loop — Memories is the “did you know X was here?” share.
What wins the room (judging):
• One polished moment > five half-built ones. Pick a hero flow, nail it.
• Wow in the first 30s — originality/wow is scored heaviest for creative builds.
• Peak-end rule — design the opening beat AND the ending. Memories replay IS the ending.
• De-risk the demo: pre-cache one rewound photo + one voice line in case mic/quota fail live.
