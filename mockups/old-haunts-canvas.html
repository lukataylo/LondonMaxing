Why kill the lens filter?
Material + NN/g: filter chips aren’t navigation and shouldn’t sit beside tabs — two nav-ish rows disorient. A 6-stop loop has too few objects to filter.
Grudge = UI event.
Telltale’s “X will remember that” toast at the trigger + a guaranteed callback at a later stop is what makes memory feel momentous. Named tiers beat a bare number.
